$ series geometric 1 2
1

4
8
16

...

$ series [OPTIONS] [TYPE] [ARGS...]
  -x              show x values
  -n              num values (or a range of values)
  -i, --integers  round output values
  -s, --sum       sum n values (or provide the series sum)

$ series geometric 1 2 -x -n 3
0 1
1 2
2 4

$ series geometric 1 2 -x -n 1...6
1 2
2 4
3 8
4 16
5 32

$ series geometric 1 2 -s
# exit 1: non-convergent series

$ series geometric 1 2 -s 3
7

$ series geometric 1 0.5 -s
2
